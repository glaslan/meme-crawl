Memes, while many of you probably recognize them as funny internet pictures, by socialogical definition they are so much more. Memes are loosely defined as units of cultural information, such as a practice or idea, that is transmitted eather verbally, or in the case of today, non-verbally through self-propagation and imitation. In today's hyperconnected world memes have an increased role in non-verbal information exchange whether useful or not.
